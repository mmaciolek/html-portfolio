The Best Anime According to Michael

My top 3 anime of all-time.

1. One Piece

This is my favorite anime of all-time. I love the characters, setting, and emotional moments. My favorite character is Nico Robin.

2. Great Teacher Onizuka

GTO is an awesome show with relatable themes. Onizuka is a man who sticks to his values no matter what, which makes him a perfect main character.

3. Golden Time

Golden Time is a fun romantic comedy full of funny moments, emotional scenes, and interesting characters.
